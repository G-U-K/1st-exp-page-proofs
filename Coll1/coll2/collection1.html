Right Line

The line broken, bent
and bonded in space, creates
three-dimensional forms.
This very simple statement v on geometry and technique
is one of Agata Bieleń’s elemental
design principles.
And the Poland based jewellery
designer makes sure to come
closer and closer to this
principle with every collection
she creates. For Spring/Summer
2016 she focusses on the optical
illusion created by fine lines
running through space, more
broken than bent or bonded.
That’s why the collection bears
the name Right Line.

Beauty
is the
harmony
of purpose
& form.

Right Line - Agata Bielen

The most simple forms
create the strongest
visual effects.

share+

It is a pleasure to observe the different forms the geometric shapes take on when draped around the arm, the neck, the finger or the ear. It is fascinating how they change their appearance when the person wearing them changes posture or position.
